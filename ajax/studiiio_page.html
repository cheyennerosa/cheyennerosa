Studiiio
UX design / Branding
Project Description
Studiiio is a collection tool and social app for Copic marker artists. The studiiio app is still in beta, and will be available on the app store in the beginning of 2017. It features a marker collection tracker of all 358 Copic colors in all four styles. Users can catalog owned markers or add them to their wishlist to buy later.
